John Entwistle

John Entwistle, the legendary bassist of The Who, was known for his use of Rickenbacker basses. His thunderous basslines and unique playing style made him a pioneer of rock bass guitar.

Lemmy Kilmister

Lemmy Kilmister, the iconic frontman of Motörhead, famously played a Rickenbacker bass throughout his career. His aggressive playing and gritty tone contributed to Motörhead's distinctive sound.

Chris Squire

Chris Squire, the founding member and bassist of Yes, relied on Rickenbacker basses to deliver the intricate and melodic basslines that became a hallmark of Yes' progressive rock sound.
